Future cars

The Honda NeuV is a mini-car equipped with artificial intelligence that is able to recognize the driver's emotions and, based on them, offer him appropriate help or advice. The entire instrument panel is presented in the form of a long touch screen. In addition, a payment terminal is provided in the car, so you can make purchases right on the way.
The new Chevrolet FNR looks a lot like a sci-fi movie and gives a glimpse into the future of our roads. Of course, like other futuristic cars, the Chevrolet FNR is equipped with an autonomous control system, and the driver can, if desired, just relax and not take part in driving their vehicles. In addition, this car has laser headlights and lambo doors.
